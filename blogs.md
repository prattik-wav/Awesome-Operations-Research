## Operations Research Blogs

- [OR in an OB World](https://orinanobworld.blogspot.com/) - Paul Rubin's blog on operations research, optimization modeling, and solver practice.
- [Yet Another Math Programming Consultant](https://yetanothermathprogrammingconsultant.blogspot.com/) - Erwin Kalvelagen's practical mathematical programming blog.
- [Michael Trick's Operations Research Blog](https://mat.tepper.cmu.edu/blog/) - Long-running OR blog from Michael Trick.
- [The OR Society Blog](https://www.theorsociety.com/resource-centre/blog/) - Articles and updates from the UK Operational Research Society.
- [INFORMS Blogs](https://www.informs.org/Blogs) - Blogs from INFORMS communities and publications.

## Optimization Blogs

- [Argonne Mathematics and Computer Science News](https://www.mcs.anl.gov/) - Optimization, computational science, and applied mathematics research news.
- [Gurobi Blog](https://www.gurobi.com/resources/?category-filter=blog) - Commercial solver blog with modeling, algorithms, and application posts.
- [MOSEK Blog](https://themosekblog.blogspot.com/) - Solver and conic optimization notes from MOSEK.
- [Optimization Online](https://optimization-online.org/) - Preprint hub for mathematical optimization.
- [SIAM News](https://sinews.siam.org/) - Applied mathematics news including optimization, operations research, and computational science.

## Analytics and Decision Science Blogs

- [Amazon Science: Operations Research and Optimization](https://www.amazon.science/research-areas/operations-research-and-optimization) - Research posts and papers from Amazon.
- [Analytics Magazine](https://www.analytics-magazine.org/) - INFORMS magazine for analytics and OR practice.
- [INFORMS Analytics Resources](https://www.informs.org/Explore/Analytics) - Analytics articles, awards, and practice resources.
- [Towards Data Science: Optimization](https://towardsdatascience.com/tagged/optimization) - Applied optimization and analytics posts; quality varies, so verify sources.
