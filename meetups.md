## Online Communities

- [INFORMS Connect](https://connect.informs.org/home) - Member discussion platform for INFORMS communities.
- [Operations Research Stack Exchange](https://or.stackexchange.com/) - Q&A site for OR models, algorithms, solvers, and practice.
- [Reddit r/OperationsResearch](https://www.reddit.com/r/OperationsResearch/) - Public discussion community for OR questions and career topics.
- [Stack Overflow: operations-research](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/operations-research) - Programming-focused OR questions.
- [The OR Society Communities](https://www.theorsociety.com/get-involved/) - UK OR Society groups, networks, and special interest communities.

## Solver and Modeling Communities

- [COIN-OR Mailing Lists](https://list.coin-or.org/mailman/listinfo) - Mailing lists for COIN-OR projects and solvers.
- [CPLEX Community](https://community.ibm.com/community/user/ai-datascience/communities/community-home?CommunityKey=ab7de0fd-6f43-47a9-8261-33578a231bb7) - IBM Decision Optimization community.
- [Gurobi Community](https://support.gurobi.com/hc/en-us/community/topics) - Community forum for Gurobi modeling and solver questions.
- [JuMP Discourse](https://discourse.julialang.org/c/domain/opt/13) - Julia optimization and JuMP discussion category.
- [MiniZinc Forum](https://groups.google.com/g/minizinc) - MiniZinc user discussion group.
- [OR-Tools Discuss](https://groups.google.com/g/or-tools-discuss) - Google group for OR-Tools users.
- [Pyomo Forum](https://groups.google.com/g/pyomo-forum) - Pyomo modeling and solver discussion group.
- [SCIP Mailing List](https://www.scipopt.org/index.php#contact) - SCIP Optimization Suite support and contact resources.

## Local and Regional Communities

- [AIRO](https://www.airo.org/) - Italian Operational Research Society.
- [CORS](https://cors.ca/) - Canadian Operational Research Society.
- [EURO Member Societies](https://www.euro-online.org/web/pages/161/member-societies) - National OR societies across Europe.
- [INFORMS Chapters](https://www.informs.org/Community/Chapters) - Regional INFORMS chapters in North America and beyond.
- [OR Society Regional Societies](https://www.theorsociety.com/get-involved/societies/) - UK regional OR groups.
- [ROADEF](https://www.roadef.org/) - French society for operations research and decision support.

## Student Communities

- [EURO Young](https://euroyoung.github.io/) - Community for young OR researchers in Europe.
- [INFORMS Doctoral Student Colloquia](https://www.informs.org/Meetings-Conferences) - Doctoral events connected to major INFORMS meetings.
- [INFORMS Student Chapters](https://www.informs.org/Community/Student-Chapters) - Student chapters for OR, analytics, and management science.
- [INFORMS Student Awards](https://www.informs.org/Recognizing-Excellence/Community-Prizes) - Student competitions and community prizes.
- [MTL-Students](https://www.mtl-students.com/) - Montreal student OR community.

## Social and Professional Networks

- [LinkedIn Operations Research Groups](https://www.linkedin.com/search/results/groups/?keywords=operations%20research) - Professional groups; evaluate activity and moderation before relying on any single group.
- [Meetup: Operations Research](https://www.meetup.com/topics/operations-research/) - Local meetup search for OR and optimization events.
- [Meetup: Analytics](https://www.meetup.com/topics/analytics/) - Analytics communities that often include OR practitioners.
