# Maintainers

This list is maintained by the community. Current maintainers review issues and
pull requests and keep the resource lists accurate.

## Current Maintainers

- [@SirMore](https://github.com/SirMore) - Project lead and primary maintainer.

## Becoming a Maintainer

Regular, high-quality contributors may be invited to become maintainers. If you
would like to help maintain a specific area (for example, a topic file or a
domain), open an issue describing how you would like to contribute. See
[CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for contribution guidelines.

## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Review pull requests for relevance, quality, formatting, and official sourcing.
- Triage issues, including broken-link and resource-suggestion reports.
- Keep the link-check workflow healthy and act on its advisory reports.
- Follow the maintenance cadence described in [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md).
