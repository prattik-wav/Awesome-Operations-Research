# Code of Conduct

This project follows the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/): be respectful, constructive, and welcoming.

## Expected Behavior

- Use inclusive and professional language.
- Focus feedback on the resource, description, link quality, and category fit.
- Assume good intent while still maintaining curation standards.
- Help contributors improve submissions when possible.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Spam, repeated promotional submissions, or deceptive links.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions that violate these expectations. Serious or repeated violations may result in blocked participation.
