## Foundations of Operations Research

- [The Theory of Games and Economic Behavior](https://press.princeton.edu/books/paperback/9780691130613/theory-of-games-and-economic-behavior) - von Neumann and Morgenstern. Foundational text for game theory and decision modeling.
- [The Application of Electronic Computing Machines to Linear Programming](https://www.rand.org/pubs/research_memoranda/RM704.html) - Dantzig. Early linear programming and simplex history.
- [The Science of Better](https://www.scienceofbetter.org/) - INFORMS resource explaining the scope and practice of OR/MS.

## Linear Programming and the Simplex Method

- [Maximization of a Linear Function of Variables Subject to Linear Inequalities](https://www.rand.org/pubs/papers/P141.html) - Dantzig. Foundational simplex method work.
- [A Polynomial Algorithm in Linear Programming](https://doi.org/10.1137/0136029) - Khachiyan. Ellipsoid method and polynomial-time LP.
- [A New Polynomial-Time Algorithm for Linear Programming](https://doi.org/10.1007/BF01580765) - Karmarkar. Interior-point breakthrough for LP.
- [Interior Point Methods for Linear Programming](https://doi.org/10.1007/BF01580763) - Wright. Survey and historical context for interior-point methods.

## Integer Programming

- [An Automatic Method for Solving Discrete Programming Problems](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.8.6.849) - Gomory. Cutting-plane methods for integer programming.
- [Branch and Bound Methods for Combinatorial Problems](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.14.4.699) - Lawler and Wood. Classic branch-and-bound survey.
- [Integer Programming](https://doi.org/10.1016/S0927-0507(05)12001-2) - Wolsey and Nemhauser handbook chapter reference.
- [Mixed Integer Programming: A Progress Report](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10107-008-0231-2) - Bixby. Solver progress and computational MIP practice.

## Network Flows

- [A New Algorithm for the Maximal Flow Problem](https://doi.org/10.1145/321694.321699) - Dinic. Blocking-flow algorithm for maximum flow.
- [An Algorithm for the Machine Calculation of Complex Fourier Series](https://doi.org/10.1090/S0025-5718-1965-0178586-1) - Cooley and Tukey. Not OR-specific, but relevant to computational foundations.
- [Flows in Networks](https://press.princeton.edu/books/paperback/9780691625393/flows-in-networks) - Ford and Fulkerson. Foundational network flow reference.
- [Theoretical Improvements in Algorithmic Efficiency for Network Flow Problems](https://doi.org/10.1145/322234.322243) - Edmonds and Karp. Polynomial augmenting path method.

## Dynamic Programming

- [The Theory of Dynamic Programming](https://doi.org/10.1090/S0002-9904-1954-09848-8) - Bellman. Foundational dynamic programming paper.
- [Markovian Decision Processes](https://doi.org/10.1287/mnsc.6.1.70) - Howard. Early formalization of MDP methods.
- [Least Squares Policy Iteration](https://doi.org/10.1162/153244303322533626) - Lagoudakis and Parr. Approximate dynamic programming and RL bridge.

## Stochastic Programming

- [Stochastic Linear Programming with Applications to Agricultural Economics](https://doi.org/10.2307/1905616) - Dantzig. Early stochastic programming formulation.
- [Stochastic Decomposition](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.39.2.264) - Higle and Sen. Decomposition methods for stochastic programs.
- [Sample Average Approximation Method for Stochastic Discrete Optimization](https://doi.org/10.1137/S1052623499363220) - Kleywegt, Shapiro, and Homem-de-Mello. SAA theory for stochastic discrete optimization.
- [Stochastic Programming](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.50.1.100.177) - Birge. Overview article.

## Robust Optimization

- [Robust Convex Optimization](https://doi.org/10.1007/BF02591823) - Ben-Tal and Nemirovski. Foundational robust convex optimization.
- [Robust Solutions of Uncertain Linear Programs](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.47.4.546) - El Ghaoui and Lebret. Robust linear optimization.
- [The Price of Robustness](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.1030.0065) - Bertsimas and Sim. Influential robust optimization model for discrete uncertainty.
- [Adjustable Robust Solutions of Uncertain Linear Programs](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10107-003-0454-y) - Ben-Tal, Goryashko, Guslitzer, and Nemirovski. Adjustable robust optimization.

## Queueing Theory

- [The Theory of Probabilities and Telephone Conversations](https://doi.org/10.1214/aoms/1177731727) - Erlang. Foundational queueing theory work.
- [On the Rational Determination of the Number of Circuits](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-642-78473-7_8) - Erlang. Classic teletraffic queueing model.
- [Queueing Systems with Heavy Tails](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.48.2.269.12380) - Survey-style reference for heavy-tailed queues.

## Simulation

- [Discrete Event Simulation: A First Course](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-030-34146-6) - Introductory simulation reference.
- [A Tutorial on Simulation in Healthcare](https://doi.org/10.1109/WSC.2007.4419645) - Healthcare simulation tutorial from Winter Simulation Conference proceedings.
- [Ranking, Selection, and Multiple Comparisons in Computer Simulation](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.42.5.888) - Goldsman and Nelson. Simulation output analysis and selection methods.

## Inventory Theory

- [The Optimum Quantity of Inventory](https://doi.org/10.2307/167018) - Harris. Economic order quantity foundation.
- [The Theory of Inventory Management](https://press.princeton.edu/books/hardcover/9780691653210/the-theory-of-inventory-management) - Arrow, Karlin, and Scarf. Foundational inventory theory.
- [Supply Chain Coordination with Contracts](https://doi.org/10.1287/mnsc.47.5.629.10485) - Cachon. Influential supply chain contracting survey.

## Scheduling

- [Sequencing Jobs Through a Two-Stage Process](https://doi.org/10.1002/nav.3800010109) - Johnson. Classic two-machine flow shop scheduling rule.
- [Scheduling Algorithms](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-662-48966-4) - Brucker. Reference for deterministic scheduling algorithms.
- [A Classification Scheme for Scheduling Problems](https://doi.org/10.1016/0377-2217(79)90042-0) - Graham, Lawler, Lenstra, and Rinnooy Kan. Standard scheduling notation.

## Vehicle Routing and Logistics

- [The Truck Dispatching Problem](https://doi.org/10.1287/mnsc.6.1.80) - Dantzig and Ramser. Original vehicle routing problem paper.
- [The Vehicle Routing Problem: Latest Advances and New Challenges](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-0-387-77778-8) - Golden, Raghavan, and Wasil, editors. Survey volume.
- [A Guided Tour of the Vehicle Routing Problem](https://doi.org/10.1016/S0377-2217(02)00310-5) - Toth and Vigo. Overview of VRP variants and methods.

## Facility Location

- [The Facility Location Problem: A Survey](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.12.3.450) - Early survey lineage for location theory.
- [Discrete Location Theory](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/book/10.1002/9781118032343) - Daskin. Standard reference for location models.
- [Location Science](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-319-13111-5) - Laporte, Nickel, and Saldanha da Gama, editors. Modern location science reference.

## Decomposition Methods

- [Partitioning Procedures for Solving Mixed-Variables Programming Problems](https://doi.org/10.1007/BF01580896) - Benders. Foundational Benders decomposition paper.
- [Decomposition Principle for Linear Programs](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.8.1.101) - Dantzig and Wolfe. Foundational column generation and decomposition.
- [Logic-Based Benders Decomposition](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10601-005-2811-9) - Hooker and Ottosson. Generalized Benders for combinatorial optimization.
- [Branch-and-Price: Column Generation for Solving Huge Integer Programs](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.46.3.316) - Barnhart, Johnson, Nemhauser, Savelsbergh, and Vance. Branch-and-price survey.

## Approximation and Heuristics

- [Analysis of a Simple 2-Approximation Algorithm for the k-Center Problem](https://doi.org/10.1016/0022-0000(85)90024-5) - Hochbaum and Shmoys. Approximation algorithm landmark.
- [The Probabilistic Method](https://www.wiley.com/en-us/The+Probabilistic+Method%2C+4th+Edition-p-9781119061953) - Alon and Spencer. Core method for randomized algorithms.
- [Optimization by Simulated Annealing](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.220.4598.671) - Kirkpatrick, Gelatt, and Vecchi. Foundational metaheuristic paper.
- [Genetic Algorithms in Search, Optimization, and Machine Learning](https://www.pearson.com/en-us/subject-catalog/p/genetic-algorithms-in-search-optimization-and-machine-learning/P200000003491) - Holland. Foundational evolutionary computation reference.

## OR and Machine Learning

- [Decision-Focused Learning: Foundations, State of the Art, Benchmark and Future Opportunities](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.13565) - Survey on integrating prediction and optimization.
- [Machine Learning for Combinatorial Optimization: A Methodological Tour d'Horizon](https://arxiv.org/abs/1811.06128) - Bengio, Lodi, and Prouvost. Survey on ML for combinatorial optimization.
- [Predict and Optimize: Through the Lens of Learning to Rank](https://doi.org/10.1145/3292500.3330856) - Elmachtoub and Grigas. Predict-then-optimize loss framework.
- [Smart "Predict, then Optimize"](https://doi.org/10.1287/mnsc.2020.3922) - Elmachtoub and Grigas. Decision-aware prediction methodology.

## Recent Surveys

- [A Survey of Algorithms for Black-Box Optimization](https://arxiv.org/abs/1903.02789) - Overview of derivative-free and black-box optimization methods.
- [A Survey on Benders Decomposition](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ejor.2019.08.003) - Modern review of Benders decomposition.
- [A Survey on Stochastic Programming](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.50.1.100.177) - Overview of stochastic programming models and methods.
- [Combinatorial Optimization with Graph Neural Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2102.09544) - Survey of GNNs for combinatorial optimization.
