## Introductory Operations Research

- [MIT 15.053 Optimization Methods in Management Science](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/15-053-optimization-methods-in-management-science-spring-2013/) - MIT OpenCourseWare. Linear, integer, nonlinear, network, and dynamic programming models.
- [MIT 15.060 Data, Models, and Decisions](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/15-060-data-models-and-decisions-fall-2014/) - MIT OpenCourseWare. Decision models, uncertainty, simulation, and optimization.
- [NPTEL Operations Research](https://nptel.ac.in/courses/112106134) - NPTEL lecture series covering classical OR models and applications.
- [Coursera Operations Research Series](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/operations-research) - National Taiwan University specialization on OR models, algorithms, and theory.
- [Georgia Tech ISyE Course Resources](https://www.isye.gatech.edu/academics/courses) - Course catalog for industrial engineering, optimization, and analytics topics.

## Linear Programming

- [Penn State MATH 484: Linear Programming](https://online.stat.psu.edu/statprogram/reviews/linear-programming) - Penn State course notes and examples.
- [Linear Optimization](https://www.hds.utc.fr/~fdandrea/LinearOptimization.html) - Fabio D'Andreagiovanni. Lecture materials on linear optimization.
- [MIT 15.053 Linear Optimization Units](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/15-053-optimization-methods-in-management-science-spring-2013/pages/lecture-notes/) - MIT lecture notes on LP modeling, simplex, and duality.
- [CMU 15-859E Linear and Semidefinite Programming](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/afs/cs.cmu.edu/academic/class/15859-f11/www/) - Advanced algorithms course on LP and SDP.

## Integer and Combinatorial Optimization

- [Coursera Discrete Optimization](https://www.coursera.org/learn/discrete-optimization) - University of Melbourne course on modeling and solving discrete optimization problems.
- [Coursera Basic Modeling for Discrete Optimization](https://www.coursera.org/learn/basic-modeling) - University of Melbourne course using MiniZinc.
- [Coursera Advanced Modeling for Discrete Optimization](https://www.coursera.org/learn/advanced-modeling) - University of Melbourne course on advanced MiniZinc modeling.
- [Coursera Solving Algorithms for Discrete Optimization](https://www.coursera.org/learn/solving-algorithms-discrete-optimization) - University of Melbourne course on search and solver methods.
- [Stanford CS261: A Second Course in Algorithms](https://web.stanford.edu/class/cs261/) - Tim Roughgarden. Approximation algorithms and algorithmic tools relevant to combinatorial optimization.
- [University of Wisconsin Integer Optimization Lectures](https://www.youtube.com/@AlbertoDelPia/playlists) - Alberto Del Pia. Integer optimization lecture playlists and materials.

## Convex Optimization

- [Stanford EE364a: Convex Optimization I](https://web.stanford.edu/class/ee364a/) - Stephen Boyd. Canonical convex optimization course.
- [Stanford EE364b: Convex Optimization II](https://web.stanford.edu/class/ee364b/) - Advanced topics in convex optimization and applications.
- [UCLA EE236B: Convex Optimization](http://www.seas.ucla.edu/~vandenbe/ee236b/ee236b.html) - Lieven Vandenberghe. Convex analysis, duality, and algorithms.
- [CMU Convex Optimization](https://www.stat.cmu.edu/~ryantibs/convexopt/) - Ryan Tibshirani. Lecture notes and materials.
- [NPTEL Convex Optimization](https://nptel.ac.in/courses/111106123) - NPTEL course on convex optimization foundations.
- [edX Convex Optimization](https://www.edx.org/learn/engineering/stanford-university-convex-optimization) - Stanford convex optimization course on edX.

## Network Optimization

- [MIT 15.053 Network Optimization Lectures](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/15-053-optimization-methods-in-management-science-spring-2013/pages/lecture-notes/) - Network flow modeling and algorithms.
- [Cornell Network Flows and Matching Notes](https://www.cs.cornell.edu/courses/cs6820/) - Advanced algorithmic material relevant to network optimization.
- [Princeton Algorithms, Part II](https://www.coursera.org/learn/algorithms-part2) - Graph algorithms, flows, and reductions useful for OR.

## Stochastic Models

- [MIT 15.070J Advanced Stochastic Processes](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/15-070j-advanced-stochastic-processes-fall-2013/) - MIT OpenCourseWare on stochastic processes.
- [MIT 6.262 Discrete Stochastic Processes](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-262-discrete-stochastic-processes-spring-2011/) - MIT OpenCourseWare on Markov chains, renewal processes, and queues.
- [Berkeley STAT 150 Stochastic Processes](https://www.stat.berkeley.edu/~aldous/150/) - Public stochastic process materials.
- [Stochastic Programming Lectures](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL9U0RhCnVfG7KMhOHPyRFlYZJ68dHeoEt) - Anthony Papavasiliou. Video lectures on stochastic programming.

## Simulation

- [MIT 15.060 Simulation Units](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/15-060-data-models-and-decisions-fall-2014/pages/lecture-notes/) - Simulation and decision analysis materials.
- [NPTEL Simulation Modeling and Analysis](https://nptel.ac.in/courses/110106046) - Simulation course covering modeling and statistical output analysis.
- [SimPy Documentation and Examples](https://simpy.readthedocs.io/) - Practical Python-based discrete-event simulation tutorials.

## Queueing Theory

- [MIT 6.262 Queueing Material](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-262-discrete-stochastic-processes-spring-2011/) - Queueing and discrete stochastic process lectures.
- [NPTEL Queueing Systems](https://nptel.ac.in/courses/111102014) - Queueing theory course materials.
- [University of Waterloo Queueing Theory Notes](https://ece.uwaterloo.ca/~stj25/queueing.html) - Public queueing notes and examples.

## Supply Chain and Inventory

- [MIT 15.763J Manufacturing System and Supply Chain Design](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/15-763j-manufacturing-system-and-supply-chain-design-spring-2005/) - MIT OpenCourseWare on supply chain and manufacturing system design.
- [MIT CTL Open Learning Materials](https://ctl.mit.edu/education/open-learning) - Supply chain education and professional learning resources.
- [Coursera Supply Chain Analytics](https://www.coursera.org/learn/supply-chain-analytics) - Rutgers course on supply chain analytics.

## Scheduling

- [Constraint Programming Course](https://www.edx.org/learn/computer-programming/universite-catholique-de-louvain-constraint-programming) - UCLouvain course with scheduling and CP applications.
- [CPAIOR Master Classes](https://cpaior.org/) - Conference master classes often cover scheduling, routing, CP, and OR-AI integration.
- [OR-Tools Scheduling Guides](https://developers.google.com/optimization/scheduling) - Practical tutorials for scheduling with CP-SAT.

## Decision Analysis

- [MIT 15.060 Decision Analysis Units](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/15-060-data-models-and-decisions-fall-2014/) - Decision trees, uncertainty, risk, and simulation.
- [Stanford CS234 Reinforcement Learning](https://web.stanford.edu/class/cs234/) - MDPs and reinforcement learning methods connected to OR.
- [Dimitri Bertsekas Dynamic Programming Lectures](https://www.youtube.com/@dimitribert/playlists) - Dynamic programming, optimal control, and reinforcement learning lectures.

## Algorithms for OR

- [MIT 6.046J Design and Analysis of Algorithms](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-046j-design-and-analysis-of-algorithms-spring-2015/) - Algorithms foundation for OR.
- [Princeton Algorithms](https://algs4.cs.princeton.edu/home/) - Algorithms textbook, code, and Coursera courses.
- [Stanford Algorithms Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/algorithms) - Algorithm design and analysis, including graph algorithms and NP-completeness.
- [Berkeley CS 270 Combinatorial Algorithms and Data Structures](https://people.eecs.berkeley.edu/~satishr/cs270/sp11/) - Advanced algorithms relevant to optimization.

## Data Science, Analytics, and OR

- [MIT 15.071 The Analytics Edge](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/15-071-the-analytics-edge-spring-2017/) - Analytics course with optimization and predictive modeling applications.
- [MIT 6.036 Introduction to Machine Learning](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-036-introduction-to-machine-learning-fall-2020/) - ML foundations useful for OR and analytics integration.
- [Berkeley CS 285 Deep Reinforcement Learning](https://rail.eecs.berkeley.edu/deeprlcourse/) - Advanced reinforcement learning course relevant to sequential decision-making.
- [Stanford CS229 Machine Learning](https://cs229.stanford.edu/) - ML course with optimization-heavy foundations.
