## Optimization Competitions

- [MIPLIB Computational Challenges](https://miplib.zib.de/) - Mixed-integer programming benchmark collections and computational challenge ecosystem.
- [ROADEF Challenge](https://www.roadef.org/challenge/2022/en/) - Industrial optimization challenge organized by the French OR society and partners.
- [PACE Challenge](https://pacechallenge.org/) - Parameterized Algorithms and Computational Experiments challenge with graph and combinatorial optimization problems.
- [GECCO Competitions](https://gecco-2024.sigevo.org/Competitions) - Evolutionary computation and optimization competitions.
- [NeurIPS Competitions](https://neurips.cc/Conferences) - Machine learning competitions that increasingly include decision-making and optimization tasks.

## Constraint Programming Competitions

- [MiniZinc Challenge](https://www.minizinc.org/challenge/) - Annual competition for constraint programming solvers using MiniZinc benchmarks.
- [XCSP Competition](https://www.cril.univ-artois.fr/XCSP/) - Competition for constraint satisfaction and optimization solvers.
- [SAT Competition](https://satcompetition.github.io/) - SAT solver competition relevant to CP, combinatorial optimization, and proof systems.
- [MaxSAT Evaluation](https://maxsat-evaluations.github.io/) - MaxSAT solver evaluation and benchmarks.

## Benchmark Challenges

- [DIMACS Challenges](https://dimacs.rutgers.edu/programs/challenge/) - Long-running algorithmic challenges covering graph algorithms, optimization, and discrete mathematics.
- [TSPLIB](http://comopt.ifi.uni-heidelberg.de/software/TSPLIB95/) - Traveling salesperson benchmark library used in exact and heuristic algorithm comparisons.
- [CVRPLIB](http://vrp.galgos.inf.puc-rio.br/index.php/en/) - Vehicle routing benchmark library.
- [Hans Mittelmann Benchmarks](https://plato.asu.edu/bench.html) - Solver benchmark results for LP, MIP, QP, conic, and nonlinear optimization.
- [QPLIB](https://qplib.zib.de/) - Quadratic programming benchmark library.

## Industry and Applied OR Challenges

- [INFORMS Franz Edelman Competition](https://www.informs.org/Recognizing-Excellence/INFORMS-Prizes/Franz-Edelman-Award) - Premier award competition for applied OR and analytics impact.
- [INFORMS Wagner Prize](https://www.informs.org/Recognizing-Excellence/INFORMS-Prizes/Daniel-H.-Wagner-Prize) - Competition for excellence in OR practice.
- [INFORMS Doing Good with Good OR](https://www.informs.org/Recognizing-Excellence/INFORMS-Prizes/Doing-Good-with-Good-OR) - Recognizes OR work with social impact.
- [Amazon Last Mile Routing Research Challenge](https://registry.opendata.aws/amazon-last-mile-challenges/) - Research challenge dataset for last-mile delivery and routing.

## Data Science Competitions with OR Content

- [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/competitions) - Hosts forecasting, scheduling, routing, allocation, and logistics-related competitions; verify OR relevance case by case.
- [DrivenData](https://www.drivendata.org/competitions/) - Social-good data competitions often involving allocation, operations, and policy decisions.
- [AIcrowd](https://www.aicrowd.com/challenges) - AI challenges that may include reinforcement learning, planning, and optimization.
- [EvalAI](https://eval.ai/) - Challenge platform for AI tasks, including planning and decision-making benchmarks.
