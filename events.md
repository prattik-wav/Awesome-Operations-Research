## Major Operations Research and Management Science Conferences

- [CORS Annual Conference](https://cors.ca/) - Canadian Operational Research Society annual meeting.
- [EURO Conference](https://www.euro-online.org/web/pages/460/calendar) - Major European conference for operational research.
- [IFORS Conference](https://ifors.org/) - Triennial global conference of the International Federation of Operational Research Societies.
- [INFORMS Annual Meeting](https://www.informs.org/Meetings-Conferences) - Large annual OR/MS conference across research, practice, and education.
- [INFORMS Business Analytics Conference](https://www.informs.org/Meetings-Conferences) - Practitioner-oriented analytics and OR conference.
- [INFORMS Healthcare Conference](https://www.informs.org/Meetings-Conferences) - Healthcare operations, analytics, and systems engineering conference.
- [OR Society Annual Conference](https://www.theorsociety.com/events/) - Annual conference of the Operational Research Society in the United Kingdom.
- [ORAHS](https://orahs.di.unito.it/) - European Working Group on Operational Research Applied to Health Services.
- [POMS Annual Conference](https://www.poms.org/) - Production and Operations Management Society conference.
- [ROADEF Congress](https://www.roadef.org/) - French OR and decision support conference.

## Optimization and Mathematical Programming Conferences

- [ACM-SIAM Symposium on Discrete Algorithms](https://www.siam.org/conferences-events/) - Major algorithms conference with strong combinatorial optimization content.
- [CPAIOR](https://cpaior.org/) - International conference on integration of constraint programming, artificial intelligence, and Operations Research.
- [Integer Programming and Combinatorial Optimization](https://www.mathopt.org/?nav=ipco) - IPCO conference on integer programming and combinatorial optimization.
- [International Symposium on Mathematical Programming](https://www.mathopt.org/) - ISMP, the flagship Mathematical Optimization Society meeting.
- [MOPTA](https://coral.ise.lehigh.edu/~mopta/) - Modeling and Optimization: Theory and Applications conference.
- [SIAM Conference on Mathematics of Data Science](https://www.siam.org/conferences-events/) - Data science, optimization, and computational mathematics.
- [SIAM Conference on Optimization](https://www.siam.org/conferences-events/) - Major optimization conference hosted by SIAM.

## Simulation Conferences

- [Winter Simulation Conference](https://meetings.informs.org/wordpress/wsc/) - Leading conference for simulation methodology and applications.
- [ACM SIGSIM PADS](https://www.acm-sigsim.org/) - Principles of Advanced Discrete Simulation conference.
- [SummerSim](https://www.scs.org/) - Summer Simulation Conference organized through the Society for Modeling and Simulation International.

## Specialized Workshops

- [Constraint Programming Conference](https://www.a4cp.org/events/cp-conference-series) - Main conference for principles and practice of constraint programming.
- [DIMACS Workshops](https://dimacs.rutgers.edu/events/) - Workshops on discrete mathematics, algorithms, optimization, and data science.
- [EURO Working Groups](https://www.euro-online.org/web/pages/1457/working-groups) - Specialized European working groups in OR subfields.
- [International Conference on Stochastic Programming](https://stoprog.org/) - Stochastic programming community conference.
- [Learning and Intelligent Optimization](https://www.lion-conference.org/) - LION conference on optimization, learning, and metaheuristics.
- [ROADEF Challenge](https://www.roadef.org/challenge/2022/en/) - Industrial optimization challenge organized by ROADEF and partners.
- [TRISTAN](https://www.tristan-symposium.org/) - Symposium on transportation analysis.
- [VeRoLog](https://verolog.eu/) - EURO Working Group on Vehicle Routing and Logistics Optimization workshops.
