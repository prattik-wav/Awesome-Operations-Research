## New to Operations Research? Start with These 5 Resources

A five-step on-ramp. Pick one item from each line and you have everything needed to build and solve your first models.

- 📚 **Book:** [Introduction to Operations Research](books.md#general-operations-research) - Broad, classic introduction to the field.
- 🆓 **Free course:** [Discrete Optimization (Coursera)](courses.md) - Hands-on introduction to modeling and solving optimization problems.
- 🧰 🆓 **Software/tool:** [OpenSolver](softwares.md) - Free spreadsheet optimizer for taking the first step beyond Excel Solver.
- 🆓 **Modeling library:** [PuLP](libraries.md#python) - Simple Python library for writing and solving linear and integer programs.
- 🧑‍🏫 **Community:** [Operations Research Stack Exchange](meetups.md) - Friendly Q&A community for OR and optimization questions.

## Best Beginner Books

- ⭐ 📚 🧑‍🏫 [Introduction to Operations Research](books.md#general-operations-research) - Good starting point for a broad OR course.
- 🔥 📚 [Operations Research: An Introduction](books.md#general-operations-research) - Widely used introductory textbook with many classical models.
- ⭐ 🆓 📚 [Applied Mathematical Programming](books.md#modeling-and-mathematical-programming) - Free MIT-hosted classic that is strong for modeling fundamentals.
- 🔥 📚 [Model Building in Mathematical Programming](books.md#modeling-and-mathematical-programming) - Excellent for learning how to formulate real optimization models.

## Best Advanced Optimization Books

- ⭐ 🎓 [Introduction to Linear Optimization](books.md#linear-and-integer-programming) - Common in graduate optimization sequences.
- ⭐ 🎓 [Integer and Combinatorial Optimization](books.md#linear-and-integer-programming) - Essential advanced integer programming reference.
- ⭐ 🆓 🎓 [Convex Optimization](books.md#nonlinear-and-convex-optimization) - Free, widely used, and foundational for convex optimization.
- 🔥 🧪 [Numerical Optimization](books.md#nonlinear-and-convex-optimization) - Strong reference for nonlinear optimization algorithms.

## Best Simulation Books

- ⭐ 📚 [Simulation Modeling and Analysis](books.md#simulation) - Standard reference for simulation methodology.
- 🔥 📚 [Discrete-Event System Simulation](books.md#simulation) - Good teaching-oriented simulation text.
- 🔥 🧪 [The Art of Computer Systems Performance Analysis](books.md#simulation) - Useful for experimental design and performance analysis.

## Best Free Online Courses

- ⭐ 🆓 📚 [MIT 15.053 Optimization Methods in Management Science](courses.md#introductory-operations-research) - Strong free OR and optimization course.
- ⭐ 🆓 📚 [Stanford EE364a Convex Optimization](courses.md#convex-optimization) - Essential convex optimization course.
- 🔥 🆓 📚 [MIT 15.071 The Analytics Edge](courses.md#data-science-analytics-and-or) - Good bridge between analytics and OR.
- 🔥 🆓 📚 [NPTEL Operations Research](courses.md#introductory-operations-research) - Broad lecture sequence for classical OR.

## Best Paid or Certificate Courses

- 🔥 📚 [Coursera Operations Research Series](courses.md#introductory-operations-research) - Certificate-friendly OR sequence.

## Best Python Libraries

- ⭐ 🧰 🆓 [Pyomo](libraries.md#python) - Flexible algebraic modeling for research and applications.
- ⭐ 🧰 🆓 [OR-Tools](libraries.md#python) - Excellent for routing, scheduling, CP-SAT, flows, and teaching examples.
- 🔥 🧰 🆓 [CVXPY](libraries.md#python) - Best starting point for convex optimization in Python.
- 🔥 🧰 🆓 [SimPy](libraries.md#python) - Practical Python library for discrete-event simulation.
- 🔥 🧰 🆓 [NetworkX](libraries.md#python) - Useful for graph modeling and network algorithms.

## Best Julia Libraries

- ⭐ 🧰 🆓 [JuMP](libraries.md#julia) - Leading Julia modeling language for optimization.
- ⭐ 🧪 🆓 [MathOptInterface](libraries.md#julia) - Solver abstraction layer that powers Julia optimization.
- 🔥 🧰 🆓 [SDDP.jl](libraries.md#julia) - Strong for stochastic dual dynamic programming.
- 🔥 🧰 🆓 [PowerModels.jl](libraries.md#julia) - Excellent for energy systems optimization.

## Best R Libraries

- 🔥 🧰 🆓 [CVXR](libraries.md#r) - Convex optimization modeling in R.
- 🔥 🧰 🆓 [simmer](libraries.md#r) - Discrete-event simulation in R.

## Best Open-Source Solvers

- ⭐ 🆓 🧰 [HiGHS](solver-comparison.md#solver-notes) - Strong open-source LP solver with growing MIP/QP capabilities.
- ⭐ 🆓 🧪 [SCIP](solver-comparison.md#solver-notes) - Research-oriented MIP/MINLP framework with strong capabilities.
- 🔥 🆓 🧰 [CBC](solver-comparison.md#solver-notes) - Practical open-source MIP baseline used by many modeling tools.
- 🔥 🆓 🧰 [OR-Tools CP-SAT](softwares.md#constraint-programming-tools) - Excellent open-source CP-SAT solver for scheduling and combinatorial models.
- 🔥 🆓 🧰 [Ipopt](softwares.md#open-source-solvers) - Widely used open-source nonlinear programming solver.

## Best Solver for Beginners

- ⭐ 🧑‍🏫 🆓 [HiGHS](solver-comparison.md#solver-notes) - Good starting point for LP and introductory MIP through Python, Julia, or command-line workflows.
- 🔥 🧑‍🏫 🆓 [CBC](solver-comparison.md#solver-notes) - Common default for teaching MIP through PuLP, Pyomo, and OpenSolver.
- 🔥 🧑‍🏫 🆓 [OR-Tools](softwares.md#open-source-solvers) - Friendly examples for routing, scheduling, assignment, and CP-SAT.
