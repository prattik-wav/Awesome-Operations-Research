## Optimization Benchmarks

- [DIMACS Challenges](https://dimacs.rutgers.edu/programs/challenge/) - Challenge datasets for graph algorithms, optimization, and discrete mathematics.
- [Hans Mittelmann Benchmarks](https://plato.asu.edu/bench.html) - Solver benchmarks for LP, MIP, QP, conic, nonlinear, and related problem classes.
- [MIPLIB](https://miplib.zib.de/) - Standard benchmark library for mixed-integer linear programming.
- [MINLPLib](https://www.minlplib.org/) - Benchmark library for mixed-integer nonlinear programming.
- [MiniZinc Challenge Benchmarks](https://www.minizinc.org/challenge/) - Constraint programming benchmark instances used in the MiniZinc Challenge.
- [Netlib LP](https://www.netlib.org/lp/data/) - Classic benchmark set for linear programming.
- [OR-Library](http://people.brunel.ac.uk/~mastjjb/jeb/info.html) - J. E. Beasley's OR benchmark library covering many combinatorial optimization problems.
- [QPLIB](https://qplib.zib.de/) - Benchmark library for quadratic programming.
- [TSPLIB](http://comopt.ifi.uni-heidelberg.de/software/TSPLIB95/) - Standard traveling salesperson and related routing benchmark library.

## Simulation and Queueing Datasets

- [Call Center Data from Technion SEELab](https://iew3.technion.ac.il/serveng/callcenterdata/) - Public call center datasets for queueing, service operations, and staffing research.
- [IEEE DataPort](https://ieee-dataport.org/) - Research data portal with simulation and systems datasets; access terms vary.
- [Kaggle Queueing and Service Datasets](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets?search=queue) - Public datasets for service-system analysis; verify provenance and licensing.
- [UCI Machine Learning Repository](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/) - Public datasets often useful for simulation input modeling and predictive analytics.

## Supply Chain and Logistics Datasets

- [Amazon Last Mile Routing Research Challenge](https://registry.opendata.aws/amazon-last-mile-challenges/) - Open data for last-mile routing and delivery research.
- [Bain Supply Chain Benchmarking Data](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets?search=supply+chain) - Searchable public supply-chain datasets; verify licensing and quality.
- [CORGIS Supply Chain Data](https://corgis-edu.github.io/corgis/) - Educational data sources that can support teaching examples.
- [Kaggle Supply Chain Datasets](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets?search=supply+chain) - Public supply-chain datasets for analytics and teaching; quality varies.

## Transportation and Routing Datasets

- [CVRPLIB](http://vrp.galgos.inf.puc-rio.br/index.php/en/) - Capacitated vehicle routing and related VRP benchmark instances.
- [DIMACS Implementation Challenges](https://dimacs.rutgers.edu/programs/challenge/) - Graph and routing challenge datasets.
- [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org/) - Open geographic data for network design, routing, and accessibility studies.
- [Solomon VRPTW Instances](https://www.sintef.no/projectweb/top/vrptw/solomon-benchmark/) - Classic vehicle routing with time windows benchmarks.
- [Transportation Networks for Research Core Team](https://github.com/bstabler/TransportationNetworks) - Transportation network datasets used in traffic assignment and network modeling.

## Healthcare OR Datasets

- [Healthcare Cost and Utilization Project](https://hcup-us.ahrq.gov/) - US healthcare utilization datasets for operations and policy research; access terms vary.
- [HealthData.gov](https://healthdata.gov/) - US health data portal for public health, policy, and operations applications.
- [MIMIC](https://physionet.org/content/mimiciv/) - Critical care database useful for healthcare operations and analytics; credentialed access required.
- [PhysioNet](https://physionet.org/) - Open physiologic and clinical datasets; licensing and access requirements vary.

## Energy Systems Optimization Datasets

- [IEEE PES Test Feeder Cases](https://cmte.ieee.org/pes-testfeeders/) - Power distribution system test feeders.
- [MATPOWER Cases](https://matpower.org/docs/ref/matpower7.1/lib/caseformat.html) - Power system optimization and power flow test cases.
- [NREL Data Catalog](https://data.nrel.gov/) - Renewable energy, grid, transportation, and building datasets.
- [PGLib-OPF](https://github.com/power-grid-lib/pglib-opf) - Power grid benchmark library for optimal power flow.
- [Power Grid Lib](https://github.com/power-grid-lib) - Benchmark libraries for power system optimization.

## Scheduling Benchmarks

- [Job Shop Scheduling Benchmark Problems](http://jobshop.jjvh.nl/) - Job shop scheduling benchmark collection.
- [OR-Library Scheduling Instances](http://people.brunel.ac.uk/~mastjjb/jeb/info.html) - Scheduling, assignment, and packing benchmark instances.
- [PSPLIB](https://www.om-db.wi.tum.de/psplib/) - Project Scheduling Problem Library.
- [Taillard Instances](http://mistic.heig-vd.ch/taillard/problemes.dir/ordonnancement.dir/ordonnancement.html) - Flow shop, job shop, and related scheduling benchmarks.

## Data Portals Useful for OR

- [data.gov](https://www.data.gov/) - US government open data portal.
- [Eurostat](https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat) - European statistics for policy, transport, labor, energy, and economic modeling.
- [OECD Data](https://data.oecd.org/) - International economic and policy data.
- [World Bank Open Data](https://data.worldbank.org/) - Global development, infrastructure, and economic datasets.
