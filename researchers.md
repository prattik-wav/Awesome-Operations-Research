## Optimization

- [George Dantzig](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Dantzig) - Stanford University and RAND. Linear programming, simplex method, decomposition, and mathematical programming foundations.
- [John von Neumann](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_von_Neumann) - Institute for Advanced Study. Game theory, minimax theorem, computing, and mathematical foundations.
- [Martin Groetschel](https://www.zib.de/members/groetschel) - Zuse Institute Berlin. Combinatorial optimization, polyhedral methods, and optimization infrastructure.
- [Arkadi Nemirovski](https://www2.isye.gatech.edu/~nemirovs/) - Georgia Tech. Convex optimization, robust optimization, and first-order methods.
- [Roger Wets](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roger_J-B_Wets) - UC Davis. Variational analysis and stochastic programming.
- [R. Tyrrell Rockafellar](https://sites.math.washington.edu/~rtr/) - University of Washington. Convex analysis, variational analysis, and optimization theory.

## Integer Programming

- [Ralph Gomory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ralph_E._Gomory) - IBM Research. Cutting planes and integer programming foundations.
- [Gerard Cornuejols](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/faculty-and-research/faculty-by-area/profiles/cornuejols-gerard.html) - Carnegie Mellon University. Integer programming, combinatorial optimization, and polyhedral theory.
- [Laurence Wolsey](https://uclouvain.be/en/directories/laurence.wolsey) - UCLouvain. Integer programming theory and modeling.
- [George Nemhauser](https://www.isye.gatech.edu/users/george-nemhauser) - Georgia Tech. Integer programming, scheduling, and combinatorial optimization.
- [Robert Bixby](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_E._Bixby) - Rice University and Gurobi. Computational linear and integer programming.
- [Thomas Rothvoss](https://sites.math.washington.edu/~rothvoss/) - University of Washington. Discrepancy, approximation, and integer programming.
- [Andrea Lodi](https://www.polymtl.ca/expertises/en/lodi-andrea) - Polytechnique Montreal. Mixed-integer programming, decomposition, and ML for optimization.

## Convex Optimization

- [Stephen Boyd](https://web.stanford.edu/~boyd/) - Stanford University. Convex optimization, control, signal processing, and open educational resources.
- [Lieven Vandenberghe](https://www.seas.ucla.edu/~vandenbe/) - UCLA. Convex optimization, semidefinite programming, and numerical methods.
- [Yurii Nesterov](https://uclouvain.be/en/directories/yurii.nesterov) - UCLouvain. Accelerated gradient methods and convex optimization complexity.
- [Michael Jordan](https://people.eecs.berkeley.edu/~jordan/) - UC Berkeley. Statistical learning, optimization, and decision systems.
- [Dimitri Bertsekas](https://web.mit.edu/dimitrib/www/home.html) - MIT. Dynamic programming, convex optimization, network optimization, and reinforcement learning.
- [Aharon Ben-Tal](https://ie.technion.ac.il/en/team/aharon-ben-tal/) - Technion. Robust optimization and convex optimization.

## Stochastic Programming

- [John Birge](https://voices.uchicago.edu/johnbirge/) - University of Chicago. Stochastic programming and financial optimization.
- [Alexander Shapiro](https://www2.isye.gatech.edu/~ashapiro/) - Georgia Tech. Stochastic programming, statistical inference, and risk.
- [Andrzej Ruszczynski](https://rutcor.rutgers.edu/andrzej-ruszczynski/) - Rutgers University. Stochastic programming, risk, and optimization theory.
- [Stein Wallace](https://www.nhh.no/en/employees/faculty/stein-wallace/) - Norwegian School of Economics. Stochastic programming modeling and applications.
- [Warren Powell](https://castle.princeton.edu/) - Princeton University. Approximate dynamic programming, sequential decision analytics, and stochastic optimization.

- [Barry Nelson](https://www.mccormick.northwestern.edu/research-faculty/directory/profiles/nelson-barry.html) - Northwestern University. Simulation output analysis, ranking, and selection.
- [James Wilson](https://www.ise.ncsu.edu/people/jrwilson/) - North Carolina State University. Simulation methodology and variance reduction.
- [Pierre L'Ecuyer](https://www.iro.umontreal.ca/~lecuyer/) - Universite de Montreal. Random number generation, simulation, and stochastic simulation.
- [Shane Henderson](https://people.orie.cornell.edu/shane/) - Cornell University. Simulation optimization, stochastic modeling, and applications.

- [Lloyd Shapley](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lloyd_Shapley) - UCLA and RAND. Game theory, matching, and cooperative games.
- [John Nash](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Forbes_Nash_Jr.) - Princeton University. Game theory and equilibrium concepts.
